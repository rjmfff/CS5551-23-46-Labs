About page provides details of the purpose or use of the application. We are learning to create an about page enabling user/reader to get insights of this page.

Lab Assignment 1 is a very interesting. It has several tasks for the team to accomplish. The tasks help us understand the basics of software development process in general. It enables us to understand the process of business management and software development.

ZenHub enables us to understand how the business requirements are captured and managed. It helps us break them down into smaller issues for efficient project management. This ensures the product/service delivered has good quality. It also helps us identify the progress of the work and identify areas of concerns and deal with them in a timely fashion. A work breakdown chart is also generated to see the progress against the schedule.

GitHub is a great web-based hosting service enabling the management of coding and version control for the same. It enables multiple developers to work on different parts of the same application and produce a quality product in a timely manner. The syncing feature is very helpful when developers are working on different aspects and committing code at different times.

Creately, yet another interesting tool for pictorial representation. It is useful in bringing the business idea take shape of how they look in reality. It helps the business analysts and user interaction designer create mock ups and wire frames of the idea to be brought to life.
